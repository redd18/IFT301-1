Interesting Stuff About Me

Well, you already know my name, but my name is Nicholas. I'm a software developer at the United States Geological Survey located in Flagstaff Arizona. I've been working there for about 2 years now and I found out about ASU online through the most unlikely of places. It happens to be an advertisement from Facebook. But given how many people like the advertisement or at least react to it I would imagine that it's making a significant impact. But less about that, more about my history. I'm from the Philippines; I was adopted when I was 2. I spent most of my time in California and then graduated from high school and moved to Flagstaff. I spent about 5 years at NAU. Eventually, I came to the realization that their program was just not for me and I decided to find something a little bit more my speed. Thus why I'm here. Right now I'm majoring in Information Technology.

I knew I wanted to get into this field around the age of 14. And why is 14 so significant to me? It was at this age that I had designed a fully-functional artificial intelligent chat robot, and from that point on it was all just downhill. I started diving into far more complex things and began involving myself more in technology and computers.
